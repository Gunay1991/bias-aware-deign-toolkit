Toolkit Overview

This toolkit is designed to help task designers integrate bias mitigation strategies into their annotation workflows. Drawing from cognitive science and our own empirical findings, this toolkit provides interactive checklists, code snippets, and design templates to support fair, accurate, and human-centered annotation practices.
